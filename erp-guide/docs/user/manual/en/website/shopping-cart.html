Shopping Cart

On the Webpage, a shopping cart is an icon that allows you to store all the things that you have earmarked for purchasing. It is a graphical representation of a shopping basket or a shopping cart that allows you to save the items you intend to buy.

This software displays the price of the product . It also displays shipping and handling charges, along with taxes, if applicable.

Shopping Cart Setup

To set up a shopping cart, go to the website module.

Website > Shopping Cart Settings

Enter Company Details and Checkout Details.

To make item available on website go to Item master.

Stock > Item

Enable Item for website.

Enter Website Specifications for Item.

Shop using Shopping Cart

Add item to shopping cart

Click on "Add to Cart" to add item to shopping cart.

Goto Checkout

Click on "Go to Cart" to checkout or on "Cart" which is on upper right side of the screen.

Checkout

Change quantity, select Shipping and Billing Address and click on "Place Order" to place the order.
