Job Applicant

You can mantain a list of People who have applied for a Job Opening.

To create a new Job Applicant go to

Human Resource > Job Applicant > New

Linking with an Email Account

You can link Job Application with an Email account. Suppose you link Job Application with an email job@example.com system shall create a New Job Applicant against each email received on the mailbox.

  • To link Email Account with Job Applicant, go to

Setup > Email Account > New

  • Enter the Email Address and the password, and select 'Enable Incoming'

  • In 'Append To' select 'Job Applicant'
